"Tum Bin" is a Hindi movie soundtrack featuring iconic songs like "Koi Fariyaad," sung by Jagjit Singh, and "Tum Bin," sung by K. S. Chithra. The soundtrack was a major success, with millions of copies sold and is still fondly remembered today. The film, released in 2001, was also a success, praised for its story, music, and cinematography.
